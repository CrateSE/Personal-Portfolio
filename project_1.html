Project 1: Sweden's Wind Turbine

  • Continue
November 28, 2023

Brief
History

The goal of this project, was to create a real-world, applicable solution to sustain Sweden's Renewable Energy demand via an optimized turbine blade that fitted various design contraints.

Time Line: Sept. 21st - Nov. 1 (2023)

 This project, jump-started our 1P13 experience and introduced us into role specific duties. As the manager of this project, I ensured a consistent pace of deadlines and distribution of work amongst the team. My duties varied widely, as I adapted to the work distribution, however I contributed heavily to the design of the wind blade and inital Gantt Chart:

Team's Work and Personal Contributions:

  I led the development of a Gantt Chart to streamline project timelines and milestones, fostering an efficient work environment. Collaborating closely with Shafer, I played a key role in the initial design of the Scenario Specific Blade, contributing creatively. Additionally, I optimized material selection for environmental impact and strength, focusing on a 50mm blade thickness. Hands-on evaluations ensured practicality and structural integrity. I spearheaded the creation of the project's first two report aspects, delivering a precise problem statement, technical objectives, and the team's MPI choices.

Reflection:

  Throughout this project, I've gained valuable insights and developed essential skills. Managing the project's timeline honed my organizational abilities, emphasizing the importance of structured group meetings for optimal efficiency. The experience of contributing to the blade design enhanced my creativity and problem-solving skills, pushing me to find a realistic yet innovative solution. Delving into material selection and hands-on evaluations improved my understanding of practical applications and structural integrity considerations. Balancing creativity with realism taught me the importance of optimization in design. Furthermore, the reflection on project dynamics highlighted the significance of maintaining a positive and enjoyable team environment. Learning to strike a balance between strict deadlines and fostering creativity emphasized the value of adaptability in project management. In summary, this experience has not only deepened my technical knowledge but has also enriched my interpersonal and project management skills, preparing me for future challenges in a variety of professional settings.
